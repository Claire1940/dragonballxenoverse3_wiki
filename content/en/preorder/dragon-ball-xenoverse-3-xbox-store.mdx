<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Systems: Soul Assist and Soul Switch">

Combat in Dragon Ball Xenoverse 3 retains the high-speed, 3D aerial battle style of its predecessors but introduces two revolutionary mechanics: **Soul Assist** and **Soul Switch**. These systems move away from simple "Z-Assists" and allow for deeper integration of the wider Dragon Ball roster into the player's custom build.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build Your Hero">
    Create a custom protagonist from various races (Saiyan, Earthling, etc.) and define your base Ki Attacks and close-quarters combat style.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip Soul Allies">
    Select Dragon Ball characters to act as your "Soul" anchors. These choices determine which Soul Assist and Soul Switch abilities are available in battle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Trigger Soul Assist">
    During combat, call upon an ally like Future Trunks or Tien to perform a coordinated energy attack without switching your main character.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Activate Soul Switch">
    Temporarily harness the "soul power" of a legendary hero. This transforms your move set and grants unique abilities based on the character's soul you have equipped.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Mechanic | Function | Strategic Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ki Attacks** | Ranged energy blasts | Distance control and burst damage |
| **Soul Assist** | Ally-powered strike | Combo extension and pressure |
| **Soul Switch** | Temporary transformation | Access to advanced skill sets |
