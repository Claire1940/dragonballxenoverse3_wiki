<Section icon={Swords} title="Gameplay Mechanics: Soul Switch & Combat Builds">

Combat in Dragon Ball Xenoverse 3 retains the signature high-speed, arena-style aerial movement of its predecessors while introducing game-changing tactical mechanics. Players will soar through massive combat arenas, utilizing close-quarters physical combos alongside devastating energy-based **Ki Attacks**. 

The two major pillars of the revamped combat system are **Soul Assist** and **Soul Switch**. Soul Assist allows you to call upon a classic Dragon Ball ally to execute coordinated, high-damage team attacks. Soul Switch, on the other hand, allows your custom protagonist to temporarily harness the soul power of a legendary fighter, altering your active skill set, stats, and combat style on the fly.
