<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Evolution: Soul Assist and Soul Switch">

The **dragon ball xenoverse 3 wishlist** has long requested deeper combat mechanics beyond simple button mashing. Dimps has responded with two core pillars: **Soul Assist** and **Soul Switch**. These systems allow players to integrate the powers of legendary Dragon Ball heroes directly into their custom character's moveset.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Customize Your Base Hero">
    Choose your race (Saiyan, Earthling, etc.) which determines your base stats and unique racial Ki attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip Soul Orbs">
    Collect souls from legendary warriors. Each soul provides specific stat boosts and a unique "Switch" ability.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Trigger Soul Assist">
    During combat, call upon a Dragon Ball ally to perform a coordinated energy attack without switching characters.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Activate Soul Switch">
    Temporarily harness the soul's power to transform your moveset. For example, switching to **Vegeta's soul** grants access to Final Flash.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The Soul Switch mechanic is not just a cosmetic change; it alters your character's combat identity for a limited time, allowing for dynamic mid-battle strategy shifts.
