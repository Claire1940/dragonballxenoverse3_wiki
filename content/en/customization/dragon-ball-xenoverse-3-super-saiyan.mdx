<Section icon={Target} title="Core Transformation Mechanics and Ki Tiers">

Understanding the Ki thresholds is vital for any player looking to master the **dragon ball xenoverse 3 super saiyan** skill. Unlike other transformations that might drain resources over time, the Super Saiyan form is a permanent state until the player is knocked out or manually deactivates it. However, the level of transformation achieved depends entirely on your current Ki bar at the moment of activation.

| Transformation Stage | Ki Required | Strike Damage | Ki Blast Damage | Damage Reduction |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Super Saiyan 1** | 300 (3 Bars) | +10% | +5% | 5% (Physical) |
| **Super Saiyan 2** | 400 (4 Bars) | +15% | +7.5% | 10% (Physical) |
| **Super Saiyan 3** | 500 (5 Bars) | +20% | +10% | 15% (Physical) |

While the damage output increases significantly at higher stages, players must account for the drastic reduction in Ki generation. In the Super Saiyan 3 form, your Ki gain from physical strikes is nearly zero, necessitating the use of specialized Super Souls or Charge skills to maintain offensive momentum.

<Callout type="warning" title="Ki Recovery Warning">
Transforming into Super Saiyan 3 makes you a powerhouse, but it effectively disables natural Ki regeneration. Ensure you have a 'Charge' skill equipped to avoid becoming a sitting duck.
</Callout>

</Section>
