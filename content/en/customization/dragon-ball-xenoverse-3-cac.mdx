The most innovative addition to the **dragon ball xenoverse 3 CAC** toolkit is the **Soul Switch** and **Soul Assist** system. This mechanic allows your custom hero to bridge the gap between their own power and the legendary strength of the Dragon Ball cast.

This system allows you to temporarily harness the "soul power" of a Dragon Ball character. This isn't just a stat boost; it grants you access to new abilities and combat styles mid-battle.
