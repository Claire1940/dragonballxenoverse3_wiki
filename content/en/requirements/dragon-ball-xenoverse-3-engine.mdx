<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics: Soul Assist and Soul Switch">

The **dragon ball xenoverse 3 engine** introduces two transformative combat systems: **Soul Assist** and **Soul Switch**. These mechanics move beyond simple stamina management, offering a layer of tactical depth that leverages the "souls" of legendary Dragon Ball warriors. Players are no longer fighting in isolation; they are harnessing the legacy of the franchise to overcome the challenges of AGE 1000.

**Soul Assist** allows a player to trigger a powerful coordinated attack with a Dragon Ball ally. This is not just a visual flair but a strategic tool to break enemy guards or extend combos. On the other hand, **Soul Switch** is a temporary transformation mechanic where the player's avatar adopts the soul power and specific abilities of a veteran fighter.
