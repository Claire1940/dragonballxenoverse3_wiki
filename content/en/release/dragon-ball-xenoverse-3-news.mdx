<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Combat Evolution: Soul Switch and Soul Assist Systems">

The combat system in this entry retains the signature high-speed aerial action of its predecessors while introducing two game-changing mechanics: **Soul Assist** and **Soul Switch**. These systems are designed to make the player feel more connected to the wider Dragon Ball universe, even when fighting solo.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Harness the Soul Power">
    During combat, players can activate the **Soul Switch** mechanic to temporarily borrow the abilities and power of a legendary hero.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Transform Your Move Set">
    Switching to a soul like **Vegeta** or **Piccolo** grants the player access to their unique signature techniques and stat bonuses for a limited duration.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute Soul Assist">
    While in combat, you can trigger a **Soul Assist**, calling upon a Dragon Ball ally to perform a devastating tag-team attack.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Master Race-Based Options">
    Choose between races like **Saiyan** or **Earthling**, as your choice now has a more profound impact on how these Soul systems interact with your base stats.
  </Step>
</Steps>

| Mechanic | Function | Combat Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Soul Switch** | Temporary transformation | Grants new abilities and stat buffs |
| **Soul Assist** | Ally-powered strike | Delivers high burst damage with a partner |
| **Ki Attacks** | Ranged energy blasts | Core offensive tool for distance control |
