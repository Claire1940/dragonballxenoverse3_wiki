<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Evolution: Soul Assist and Soul Switch">

Combat in **Dragon Ball Xenoverse 3** retains the signature high-speed 3D action of the series but introduces layers of tactical depth through two primary new systems. These mechanics are designed to make the player feel more connected to the legendary roster of the franchise while maintaining the focus on their custom avatar.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Avatar Customization">
    Create your hero from various races (including Saiyan and Earthling). Your choice of race significantly impacts your base combat options and transformation paths.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Exploration & Movement">
    Soar through the skies of West City and use the new boost mechanics to navigate large-scale battlefields seamlessly.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Engaging Soul Assist">
    Trigger a **Soul Assist** to call upon a Dragon Ball ally for a devastating dual attack. This mechanic rewards players for building relationships with NPC mentors.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Mastering Soul Switch">
    The **Soul Switch** system allows you to temporarily harness the soul power of a legendary fighter, granting you their specific abilities and a massive stat boost.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The Soul Switch mechanic is particularly noteworthy, as it allows for mid-battle playstyle shifts. By equipping different "Souls," your custom character can adapt to various enemy types.

| Soul Switch Example | Combat Benefit |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Vegeta Soul** | Focused on aggressive Ki blasts and high pressure |
| **Piccolo Soul** | Enhanced defensive capabilities and reach |
| **Future Trunks Soul** | Specialized in rapid sword strikes and mobility |
| **Krillin Soul** | Strategic utility and support-based Ki attacks |
| **Tien Soul** | High-risk, high-reward precision strikes |

<Callout type="tip" title="Strategic Switching">
Soul Switch is not just a power boost; it changes your move set. Use the **Piccolo Soul** when facing overwhelming numbers to utilize his area-of-effect techniques, then switch to **Vegeta** to finish off a single powerful boss.
</Callout>

</Section>
