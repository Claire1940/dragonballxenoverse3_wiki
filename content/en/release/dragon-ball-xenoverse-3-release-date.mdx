<Section icon={Swords} title="Gameplay and Combat Systems">

The core combat of Dragon Ball Xenoverse 3 retains the high-speed, three-dimensional action that defined its predecessors while introducing game-changing tactical mechanics. Battles take place across expansive environments, requiring players to master aerial movement, rapid boosting, and precise positioning.

In addition to standard close-quarters combat and energy-based Ki Attacks, players must learn to utilize two newly introduced systems: Soul Assist and Soul Switch.

  <Step num="1" title="Create Your Custom Hero">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Master Traversal and Movement">
    Learn to soar, dash, and boost through the vertical spaces of West City and various combat arenas to maintain positional advantages.

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute a Soul Switch">
    Temporarily harness the soul of a legendary fighter to alter your moveset, gain stat boosts, and turn the tide of difficult battles.
  </Step>

These mechanics add a layer of strategy to the fast-paced action. Soul Assist allows for seamless combo extensions, while Soul Switch provides temporary access to specialized fighting styles based on legendary Dragon Ball characters.

| Combat Mechanic | Input / Trigger | Tactical Benefit |
| Ki Attacks | Energy-based commands | Ranged pressure and guard breaks |
| High-Speed Boost | Traversal and dodge inputs | Rapid repositioning and evasion |
| Soul Assist | Ally command trigger | Coordinated dual strikes with active partners |
| Soul Switch | Soul gauge activation | Temporary moveset modification and stat buffs |

<Callout type="warning" title="Combat Resource Management">
