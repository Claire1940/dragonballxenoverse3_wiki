<Section icon={Swords} title="New Combat Systems: Soul Assist and Soul Switch">

Combat in the series has always been defined by its high-speed aerial maneuvers and explosive Ki attacks, but this entry introduces two revolutionary systems that change the flow of battle. These mechanics allow the player-created protagonist to draw directly upon the legacy of Dragon Ball legends.

The **Soul Assist** system is designed for collaborative offense. By triggering this mechanic, players can call upon a powerful ally to perform a synchronized attack. Unlike standard Z-Assists in other titles, Soul Assist is integrated into the combo strings, allowing for seamless transitions between your custom hero's strikes and the ally's signature move.

| Mechanic | Function | Combat Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Soul Assist** | Summon ally for a dual attack | Increases combo damage and pressure |
| **Soul Switch** | Temporarily harness a hero's soul | Grants new abilities and stat boosts |
| **Ki Attacks** | Energy-based ranged offense | Core damage dealer for all races |
| **Aerial Boost** | High-speed battlefield traversal | Essential for positioning and evasion |

The **Soul Switch** mechanic is even more transformative. It allows your character to temporarily "channel" the soul of a veteran warrior. This doesn't just change your stats; it temporarily replaces certain skill slots with the signature moves of the chosen character. Official sources have confirmed several "Soul" examples that will be available to players.
