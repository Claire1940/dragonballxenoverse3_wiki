<Section icon={Sparkles} title="How to Activate Future Trunks Soul Switch">

Activating the Soul Switch mechanic requires tactical precision during high-speed combat. Players cannot simply transform at the start of a match; instead, they must build up their resources and execute a coordinated sequence to borrow Future Trunks' physical abilities and sword techniques [1.1].

The combat loop revolves around two distinct phases: Soul Assist and Soul Switch [1.1]. Soul Assist allows you to summon Future Trunks for a brief, powerful joint attack, while Soul Switch temporarily swaps your custom avatar's moveset with Trunks' signature style [1.1].

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build the Soul Gauge">
    Engage in active combat by landing basic physical strikes and executing successful Ki attacks. Defending against enemy assaults also contributes to charging your Soul Gauge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Trigger Soul Assist">
    Once the gauge reaches the midway point, initiate a Soul Assist [1.1]. This summons Future Trunks to deliver a coordinated strike alongside your custom hero, staggering the opponent [1.1].
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute the Soul Switch">
    When the Soul Gauge is fully charged, activate the Soul Switch command [1.1]. Your custom character will temporarily harness Trunks' soul power, shifting your active moveset to his sword-based style [1.1].
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unleash Signature Ki Attacks">
    While the transformation is active, utilize Trunks' high-speed sword combos and devastating energy attacks to overwhelm your opponent before the gauge depletes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="tip" title="Stagger Strategy">
Always activate your Soul Assist right before executing a full Soul Switch [1.1]. The assist attack creates a reliable stagger window, ensuring your transformation animation cannot be interrupted by aggressive opponents.
</Callout>

Understanding the differences between these two systems is key to mastering the flow of battle:

| Mechanic Type | Resource Cost | Active Duration | Primary Combat Benefit |

| **Soul Assist** | Half Soul Gauge | Instantaneous | Coordinated double-team strike, high stagger potential |
| **Soul Switch** | Full Soul Gauge | Temporary | Complete moveset transformation, enhanced sword combos |
