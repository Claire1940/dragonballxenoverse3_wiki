<Section icon={Swords} title="Mastering the Soul Switch Mechanic">

The most significant update to the **dragon ball xenoverse 3 characters** system is the **Soul Switch** mechanic. This allows players to temporarily harness the "soul power" of legendary warriors to gain new abilities, stat boosts, and signature moves during high-speed action sequences. This system effectively integrates the massive roster of the Dragon Ball franchise into the player's custom build without requiring them to switch characters entirely.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Equip a Soul">
    Before entering battle, players select a "Soul" from their collected roster of Dragon Ball allies, such as **Vegeta** or **Future Trunks**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Build Energy">
    Engage in close-quarters combat and Ki attacks to fill the Soul Gauge during the heat of battle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Activate Soul Switch">
    Trigger the mechanic to temporarily transform your moveset. For example, switching to **Piccolo's** soul might grant access to the Special Beam Cannon.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Utilize Soul Assist">
    While a soul is active, you can trigger a **Soul Assist**, where the ally briefly appears to perform a powerful dual attack with your protagonist.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Official news from June 2026 confirmed that **Krillin, Piccolo, Vegeta, Future Trunks, and Tien** are among the first batch of characters available for the Soul Switch mechanic. Each offers a distinct tactical advantage, making "Soul Collecting" a primary objective for players looking to optimize their combat builds.
