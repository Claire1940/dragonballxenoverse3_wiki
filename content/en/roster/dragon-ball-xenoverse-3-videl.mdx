<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step: Unlocking All Videl Variations">

Unlocking the full potential of **Dragon Ball Xenoverse 3 Videl** requires navigating the new mission-based progression system. Unlike Xenoverse 2, where many characters were unlocked via the main story, Xenoverse 3 utilizes "Timeline Shards" found in the hub world.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Complete the 'Satan City' Prologue">
    Defeat the initial wave of timeline invaders in the Satan City district to unlock Videl's base 'Short Hair' variation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Collect 5 Timeline Shards">
    Search the far-future hub world for shards. These are often hidden behind destructible objects like ice shards or building debris.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Master the Soul-Taking Ritual">
    Interact with the Elder Kai in the messy room hub. This unlocks the ability to use Videl's soul, granting her Pigtails variation.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Defeat Broly in the Ice Level">
    Complete the high-intensity boss fight against Broly. Using Videl as an assist during this fight unlocks her 'Broly - Second Coming' dress.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Max Friendship with Gohan">
    Participate in 20 missions with Future Gohan as your mentor to unlock the Great Saiyaman 2 (Saiyawoman) transformation.
  </Step>
</Steps>
