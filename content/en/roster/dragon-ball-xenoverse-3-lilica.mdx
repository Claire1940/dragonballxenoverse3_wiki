While **dragon ball xenoverse 3** retains the core action-packed gameplay of its predecessors, it introduces two revolutionary systems: **Soul Switch** and **Soul Assist**. These mechanics are essential for mastering the difficult encounters found in the AGE 1000 story.

The **Soul Switch** mechanic allows players to temporarily harness the soul power of legendary Dragon Ball characters. This isn't just a stat boost; it changes the player's available abilities and combat style for a limited duration. For example, switching to the soul of Vegeta might grant access to the Final Flash, while a Piccolo soul switch could provide enhanced defensive capabilities.

| Mechanic | Description | Player Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
