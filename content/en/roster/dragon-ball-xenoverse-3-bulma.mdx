<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Evolution: Soul Assist and Soul Switch Systems">

Dragon Ball Xenoverse 3 introduces two major tactical layers to its high-speed action: **Soul Assist** and **Soul Switch**. These systems allow the player to leverage the power of legendary Dragon Ball warriors without requiring them to be physically present in every battle. This ensures the focus remains on your custom protagonist while maintaining a deep connection to the franchise's iconic roster.

<CardGrid cols={2}>
  <Card title="Soul Assist">

    - **Offensive Burst**: High-damage maneuvers triggered during combos.
    - **Strategic Timing**: Requires meter management for maximum efficiency.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Soul Switch">
    - **Temporary Power**: Harness the soul of a hero for a short duration.
    - **New Abilities**: Gain access to specific movesets (e.g., Piccolo's reach).
    - **Stat Buffs**: Significant boosts to Ki or physical attributes.
  </Card>
