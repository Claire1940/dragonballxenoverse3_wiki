<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering Combos and Stamina Breaks">

To excel with **dragon ball xenoverse 3 krillin**, you must master his unique combo shortcuts. His Light String (9L) is particularly noteworthy for its multi-hitting kicks and teleportation finishers. Learning when to cancel these animations into a Stamina Break is the difference between a novice and a master.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="The Light String Shortcut">
    Execute the Light String up to the sixth input (L L L L L L). This is a multi-hit kick that provides a generous window to transition into a Stamina Break.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="The Teleport Cancel">
    During the eighth attack of the Light String, Krillin teleports behind the opponent. Instead of finishing the kick, immediately input a Skill or a Block to reset your neutral position.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Heavy Loop Execution">
    The first four attacks of Krillin's Heavy String (H H H H) can be looped with precise timing. This is ideal for building Ki without expending resources.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="The Ultimate Connection">
    Perform the entire Alternating String (H L H L H) while on the floor. The final hard knockdown allows you to connect **Scatter Kamehameha** without needing a Stamina Break.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Combo Mastery">
By using the **Kamehameha Boost** during a Stamina Break, you can loop the attack into itself for devastating damage that rivals even some of the Saiyan-tier characters.
</Callout>
