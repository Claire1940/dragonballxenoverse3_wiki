<Section icon={Zap} title="Soul Switch and Combat Mechanics">

Combat in the third installment retains the high-speed aerial action the series is known for but introduces the **Soul Switch** system. This mechanic allows the player's custom hero to temporarily harness the "soul power" of legendary fighters. This isn't just a stat boost; it alters your move set and abilities to match the chosen soul.

**Key Combat Pillars:**
- **High-Speed Action**: Enhanced boosting and aerial maneuvering across larger maps.
- **Ki Attacks**: A diverse array of energy-based projectiles and cinematic finishers.
- **Soul Assist**: Triggering a synchronized attack with a Dragon Ball ally during a combo.
- **Soul Switch**: A temporary transformation state that grants the skills of a specific legend.

| Soul Ability | Example Character | Combat Effect | Duration |
