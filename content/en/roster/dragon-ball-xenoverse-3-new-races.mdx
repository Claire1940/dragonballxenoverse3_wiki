<Section icon={Shield} title="Soul Switch and Soul Assist Mechanics">

The combat system in Dragon Ball Xenoverse 3 introduces two revolutionary mechanics: **Soul Assist** and **Soul Switch**. These systems allow players to bridge the gap between their custom protagonist and the legendary heroes of the Dragon Ball universe.

### Soul Switch Examples
Soul Switch allows you to temporarily harness the power of a legendary character's soul, granting you their signature moves and a massive stat boost for a limited duration.

| Character Soul | Primary Benefit | Signature Move |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Vegeta** | Increased Offense | Final Flash |
| **Future Trunks** | Sword-based Combos | Burning Attack |
| **Piccolo** | Tactical Defense | Special Beam Cannon |
| **Krillin** | Support & Utility | Destructo-Disc |
| **Tien** | Precision Strikes | Tri-Beam |

<Callout type="success" title="Combat Strategy">
Use Soul Switch during boss encounters or high-pressure PvP moments. Timing the switch can turn the tide of a battle by giving you access to invincibility frames or devastating ultimate attacks.
</Callout>

### Soul Assist System
Unlike the temporary nature of the Switch, **Soul Assist** is a permanent ally presence. By equipping a Soul Assist, you can trigger coordinated attacks where your ally appears briefly to strike the enemy alongside you. This reinforces the "Great Saiya Squad" theme of teamwork and collective strength.

| System Component | Function | Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Soul Assist** | Ally-powered follow-up | Automatic or Triggered |
| **Soul Switch** | Temporary transformation | Manual Activation |
| **Ki Attacks** | Core energy offense | Resource Dependent |
| **High-Speed Action** | Evasive maneuvers | Stamina Dependent |
