<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Evolution: Soul Assist and Soul Switch">

The combat in **Dragon Ball Xenoverse 3 Age 1000** retains the signature high-speed aerial action of the series but introduces two revolutionary systems that change how builds are constructed. These mechanics allow your custom avatar to bridge the gap between their own power and the legendary strength of past warriors.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initialize Soul Assist">
    During combat, you can trigger a Soul Assist. This summons a projection of a Dragon Ball ally to perform a synchronized attack, dealing massive burst damage or providing defensive cover.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Build Soul Energy">
    By performing combos and successful Ki attacks, you fill your Soul Meter. Once full, you gain access to the Soul Switch ability.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Activate Soul Switch">
    Temporarily transform your fighting style by harnessing a specific hero's soul. For example, switching to Vegeta's soul grants access to the Final Flash and aggressive Saiyan rush maneuvers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Ultimate Finishers">
    While in the Soul Switch state, you can perform a unique finisher that combines your avatar's energy with the legendary hero's signature move.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The impact of your chosen race—whether Saiyan, Earthling, or others—will dictate how these souls interact with your base stats. For instance, a Saiyan protagonist might gain additional transformation tiers when Soul Switching with Vegeta, whereas an Earthling might focus on utility and Ki efficiency.

| Mechanic | Function | Best Use Case |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ki Attacks** | Ranged energy blasts | Breaking guard / Long-range pressure |
| **Soul Assist** | Ally-powered strike | Extending combos / Defensive breaks |
| **Soul Switch** | Temporary transformation | Turning the tide / Finishing bosses |
| **High-Speed Boost** | Rapid battlefield traversal | Closing distance / Dodging Ultimates |

<Callout type="success" title="Combat Mastery">
Mastering the timing between a Soul Assist and your own Ki Attacks is the key to achieving "S" rank in the new Age 1000 mission structure.
</Callout>
