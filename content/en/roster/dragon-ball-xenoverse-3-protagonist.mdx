The foundation of any **Dragon Ball Xenoverse 3 protagonist** build lies in the selection of their race. | Race | Combat Focus | Key Advantage | Confirmed Details |

The combat system introduces two revolutionary pillars: **Soul Assist** and **Soul Switch**. Soul Assist allows the protagonist to call upon a Dragon Ball ally for a devastating dual attack. Soul Switch, however, is a deeper system where the player temporarily "borrows" the soul of a legendary fighter to alter their move set and power level.

**Confirmed Soul Switch Examples:**
- **Vegeta**: Grants aggressive Ki blasts and Final Flash capabilities.
- **Piccolo**: Focuses on tactical strikes and defensive maneuvering.
- **Future Trunks**: Enhances sword-based combat and rapid movement.
- **Krillin**: Provides utility-based Ki attacks and support options.
- **Tien**: Offers high-precision strikes and unique tri-beam techniques.

<Callout type="success" title="Combat Synergy">
Combining a Saiyan protagonist's natural power with a Soul Switch like Vegeta creates a high-damage "Glass Cannon" build, whereas an Earthling using Piccolo's soul provides a more balanced, survivable playstyle.
</Callout>

While the game is still in development for its 2027 launch, early information points toward an "extensive character customization" suite. The **Dragon Ball Xenoverse 3 protagonist** will progress through a multi-layered skill tree that integrates both traditional level-ups and "Soul Resonance."
