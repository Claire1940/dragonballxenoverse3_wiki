<Section icon={Swords} title="The Soul Switch Combat System">

The most innovative way the **dragon ball xenoverse 3 roster** is utilized is through the **Soul Switch** and **Soul Assist** systems. Instead of simply selecting a character from a list, players "harness the soul" of legendary warriors to enhance their custom protagonist's abilities.

Soul Switch is a temporary transformation or power-up state. During battle, you can trigger a Soul Switch to gain the move set, strength, and unique attributes of a specific Dragon Ball icon. This allows for dynamic combat where your custom hero can suddenly unleash the tactical genius of Piccolo or the raw aggression of Vegeta.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Equip a Soul">
    Before entering a mission, select a "Soul" from your collected roster of Dragon Ball allies.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Build the Gauge">
    Engage in high-speed action and Ki attacks to fill your Soul Meter during the fight.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Activate Soul Switch">
    Trigger the mechanic to temporarily gain the abilities and soul power of the equipped character.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Soul Assist">
    Use the Soul Assist feature to call in a secondary ally for a powerful dual-team attack.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="System Limitation">
Soul Switch is temporary. You must manage your Soul Meter carefully, as losing the transformation in the middle of a high-speed aerial battle can leave you vulnerable to Ki attacks.
</Callout>
