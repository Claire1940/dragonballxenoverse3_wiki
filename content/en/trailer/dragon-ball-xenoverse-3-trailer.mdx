<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics: Soul Assist and Soul Switch">

Combat in **dragon ball xenoverse 3** retains the signature high-speed action of the series while introducing tactical layers that emphasize the bond between the player and legendary Dragon Ball heroes. The trailer highlights two revolutionary systems: **Soul Assist** and **Soul Switch**. These mechanics are designed to make the "Custom Hero" feel more integrated into the wider power scale of the franchise.

**Soul Assist** allows players to call upon a powerful ally for a coordinated strike. This isn't just a simple assist move; it's a cinematic team-up attack that can turn the tide of a difficult encounter. On the other hand, **Soul Switch** is a transformative mechanic. It allows the player's avatar to temporarily harness the "soul power" of a classic character, granting them access to unique move sets and heightened stats for a limited duration.

| Mechanic | Function | Combat Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Soul Assist** | Coordinated Ally Attack | High burst damage and combo extension |
| **Soul Switch** | Temporary Soul Harnessing | Access to unique skills (e.g., Final Flash) |
| **Ki Attacks** | Energy-based Offense | Ranged pressure and iconic special moves |
| **High-Speed Action** | Movement & Boosting | Evasion and maintaining combat momentum |

The trailer specifically names several characters whose souls can be utilized through the Soul Switch system. This adds a layer of "build-crafting" where players must decide which hero souls complement their custom character's base stats and race.
