<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics: Soul Assist and Soul Switch">

The core combat loop retains the signature high-speed arena fighting style of the series while introducing two groundbreaking tactical systems: Soul Assist and Soul Switch. These mechanics add a layer of strategy to battles, moving beyond simple button-mashing combos to emphasize team synergy and resource management.

Soul Assist allows players to summon a companion from the Dragon Ball universe to unleash a devastating synchronized attack. This mechanic is ideal for extending combos or interrupting aggressive enemy behavior. Soul Switch, on the other hand, allows the player to temporarily harness the soul power of a legendary warrior, altering their moveset and granting access to unique elemental properties and passive buffs.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Build the Soul Gauge">
    Engage in standard close-quarters combat and execute successful deflections to accumulate energy in your specialized Soul Gauge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Your Target Soul">
    Equip up to two character souls before battle. During combat, use the selection wheel to highlight the desired ally soul.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Activate Soul Switch">
    Trigger the mechanic to temporarily alter your protagonist's combat style, gaining immediate access to the chosen character's signature techniques.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Soul Assist Synergy">
    While active, call upon your secondary equipped soul to perform a dual-energy blast, maximizing damage during critical combat windows.
  </Step>
</Steps>

To understand how these systems differ and complement each other during combat, refer to the comparison below:

| Combat System | Activation Requirement | Duration | Primary Combat Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Soul Assist** | Partial Soul Gauge segment | Instantaneous | Combo extension and crowd control |
| **Soul Switch** | Full Soul Gauge depletion | Temporary limit | Complete moveset alteration and stat boosts |
| **Ki Attacks** | Standard Ki energy | Continuous | Ranged pressure and shield depletion |

<Callout type="tip" title="Synergy Strategy">
Always activate Soul Switch when an enemy's guard is broken. The temporary stat boost significantly amplifies the damage of your subsequent Soul Assist ultimate attacks.
</Callout>
