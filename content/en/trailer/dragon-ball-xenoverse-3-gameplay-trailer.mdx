<Section icon={Swords} title="Soul Switch and Combat Mechanics">

Combat in this installment retains the signature high-speed, aerial combat of the Xenoverse series while introducing two major mechanical innovations: **Soul Assist** and **Soul Switch**. These systems allow players to integrate the strength of legendary Dragon Ball warriors directly into their custom character's moveset, creating a highly dynamic flow of battle.

The gameplay trailer demonstrates how these systems function in real-time encounters. Players can execute rapid close-quarters strikes, transition into high-yield Ki Attacks, and then seamlessly call upon a companion's spirit to turn the tide of a difficult boss fight.
