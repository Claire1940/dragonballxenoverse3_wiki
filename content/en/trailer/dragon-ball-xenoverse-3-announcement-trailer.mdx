<Section icon={Swords} title="New Combat Mechanics: Soul Assist and Soul Switch">

Combat in this entry retains the signature high-speed, aerial flow of the series while introducing two major mechanical innovations: Soul Assist and Soul Switch. These systems are designed to make battles more dynamic, encouraging players to coordinate with allies and adapt their strategies mid-fight.

The gameplay trailer showcases players soaring through West City and boosting across massive battlefields, executing rapid-fire close combat strikes and devastating energy attacks. The integration of ally-based systems adds a layer of tactical depth to the fast-paced action.

  <Step num="4" title="Activate Soul Switch">

  </Step>

The Soul Switch mechanic is particularly noteworthy, as it allows players to temporarily borrow the power and movesets of legendary fighters. Confirmed examples of characters available for Soul Switch include Krillin, Piccolo, Vegeta, Future Trunks, and Tien. This system ensures that even while playing a custom hero, players can seamlessly integrate the iconic techniques of classic characters into their strategy.

| Combat System | Primary Function | Tactical Advantage |
|---------------|------------------|--------------------|
| Ki Attacks | Energy-based offensive strikes | Ideal for keeping distance and dealing burst damage |
| Soul Assist | Synchronized strike with a chosen ally | Delivers massive team-up damage to break defenses |
| Soul Switch | Temporary soul channeling system | Grants access to legendary movesets mid-combat |
| High-Speed Boost | Rapid battlefield traversal and dodging | Crucial for positioning and avoiding enemy combos |
