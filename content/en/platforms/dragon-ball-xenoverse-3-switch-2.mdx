Combat in this sequel retains the high-speed, aerial action that defined the series while introducing deep tactical layers. Players can soar through West City and boost across massive battlefields, combining physical strikes with devastating energy-based attacks. The core of the new system relies on two primary mechanics: Soul Assist and Soul Switch.

Additionally, character customization has been expanded. The race you select for your created protagonist—such as Saiyan or Earthling—directly impacts your fundamental combat options, transformation trees, and general playstyle.
