<Section icon={Swords} title="Advanced Combat: Soul Switch and Soul Assist">

The core gameplay loop of **dragon ball xenoverse 3 xbox** retains the signature high-speed action of the series but introduces two revolutionary systems: **Soul Assist** and **Soul Switch**. These mechanics are designed to deepen the tactical layer of battles, moving beyond simple combo strings into soul-based energy management.

**Soul Assist** allows players to summon the essence of a legendary Dragon Ball ally to perform a synchronized attack. This is not just a simple assist move; it scales with the player's bond with that specific character. **Soul Switch**, on the other hand, is a temporary transformation mechanic where the player's protagonist harnesses the soul power of a veteran hero to gain their unique abilities and stat boosts.

| Mechanic | Function | Confirmed Example Characters |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Soul Assist** | Tag-team energy attacks | Piccolo, Krillin, Tien |
| **Soul Switch** | Temporary soul transformation | Vegeta, Future Trunks, Piccolo |
| **Ki Attacks** | Energy-based ranged offense | Customizable per build |
| **Aerial Boost** | High-speed battlefield traversal | Universal movement mechanic |

<Callout type="warning" title="Soul Switch Limitations">
Soul Switch is a temporary state. Based on the 2026 gameplay trailer, the duration is limited by a "Soul Gauge" that refills through successful close-quarters combat. Managing this gauge is critical for high-level Xbox online play.
</Callout>
