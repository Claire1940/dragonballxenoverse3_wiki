<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Systems: Soul Assist and Soul Switch">

Dragon Ball Xenoverse 3 introduces two revolutionary systems that change the flow of high-speed aerial combat: **Soul Assist** and **Soul Switch**. These systems allow your custom protagonist to leverage the power of established Dragon Ball heroes in different ways. Soul Assist focuses on coordinated attacks, while Soul Switch provides a temporary transformation into a legendary warrior's combat style.

| Mechanic | Function | Combat Application | Key Examples |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Soul Assist** | Ally Attack | Summon an ally for a powerful combo | Bulma, Gamma 1 support |
| **Soul Switch** | Soul Harness | Temporarily gain a hero's abilities | Vegeta, Piccolo, Tien |
| **Ki Attacks** | Energy Offense | Ranged and burst damage projectiles | Kamehameha, Galick Gun |
| **High-Speed Action** | Traversal | Soaring and boosting across battlefields | Aerial dogfights, city flight |

These mechanics are essential for both solo play and the cooperative missions found in the Great Saiya Squad questline. Mastering the timing of a Soul Switch can turn the tide of a difficult boss encounter in the AGE 1000 story.
