<Section icon={Swords} title="Advanced Combat Systems: Soul Assist and Soul Switch">

The core combat of the series retains its signature high-speed aerial movement and explosive Ki attacks, but introduces two game-changing mechanics: **Soul Assist** and **Soul Switch**. These mechanics allow players to seamlessly blend the power of legendary Dragon Ball characters into their custom protagonist's combat style, creating highly personalized combo routes and tactical options.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Combat Race">
    Choose your starting race, such as Saiyan or Earthling. This choice determines your baseline combat attributes, physical combo structures, and unique racial transformation paths.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Equip Soul Assist Allies">
    Assign a powerful Dragon Ball ally to your support slot. During combat, trigger Soul Assist to summon them for devastating, synchronized team-up attacks that break enemy guards.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Activate Soul Switch">
    Harness the soul power of a legendary fighter mid-battle. Activating Soul Switch temporarily alters your protagonist's move set and grants access to exclusive, high-tier abilities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Unleash Ultimate Ki Attacks">
    Combine your temporary soul powers with physical combos to create openings, then finish the encounter with massive energy-based Ki attacks.
  </Step>
</Steps>
