<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Combat Mechanics: Soul Assist and Soul Switch">

Combat in Dragon Ball Xenoverse 3 retains the high-speed, arena-style aerial combat that defined its predecessors while introducing game-changing tactical layers. The core combat flow balances standard physical strikes, high-speed boosts, and devastating Ki Attacks. However, the true innovation lies in how players interact with their allies through the Soul Assist and Soul Switch systems.

The Soul Assist mechanic allows players to summon a legendary Dragon Ball ally to perform a coordinated strike or defensive maneuver mid-combo, keeping the pressure on the opponent. For more decisive moments, the Soul Switch mechanic lets the player temporarily channel the soul of a veteran fighter, altering their moveset, stats, and special abilities to turn the tide of battle.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate Close Combat or Ki Offense">
    Engage the target with high-speed physical combos, utilizing rapid boosts to bypass enemy defenses or pressure them with long-range Ki blasts.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Trigger Soul Assist for Combo Extension">
    Summon an ally like Future Trunks or Tien to strike the target, keeping the enemy staggered and allowing you to reposition or charge your energy.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Activate Soul Switch for Peak Power">
    Harness the soul of a legendary fighter such as Vegeta or Piccolo, temporarily replacing your standard skill set with their signature ultimate attacks.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute a Finishing Ultimate Attack">
    Unleash a high-damage energy blast while in the Soul Switch state to maximize damage before reverting to your custom avatar's base form.
  </Step>
</Steps>
