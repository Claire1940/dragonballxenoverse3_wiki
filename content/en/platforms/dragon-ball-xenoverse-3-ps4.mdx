<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics: Soul Assist and Soul Switch">

Combat in **Dragon Ball Xenoverse 3** evolves the signature high-speed action of the series. While Ki attacks and aerial maneuvers return, two new systems redefine how players interact with the legendary roster of the Dragon Ball universe: **Soul Assist** and **Soul Switch**.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Initiate Combat Flow">
    Engage enemies with close-quarters combat and standard Ki attacks to build your energy meter and positioning.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Trigger Soul Assist">
    Call upon a Dragon Ball ally to perform a coordinated strike. This doesn't just add damage; it can break enemy guards and create openings for ultimates.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Activate Soul Switch">
    Temporarily harness the "soul power" of a legendary hero. This transforms your move set and grants unique passive buffs based on the character chosen.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Finisher">
    Combine your customized protagonist's powers with the borrowed soul's abilities to unleash devastating finishing moves.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The Soul Switch system is particularly deep, allowing for tactical flexibility mid-fight. By selecting different "souls," players can pivot from a defensive stance to an all-out offensive one depending on the enemy's behavior.

| Soul Type | Example Character | Primary Benefit | Combat Style |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tactical** | **Piccolo** | Increased Defense/Regen | Mid-range Control |
| **Aggressive** | **Vegeta** | Higher Ki Damage | Close-quarters Rush |
| **Speed** | **Future Trunks** | Enhanced Evasion/Dash | Hit and Run |
| **Support** | **Krillin** | Fast Energy Recovery | Resource Management |
| **Versatile** | **Tien** | Balanced Stats | All-rounder |

<Callout type="success" title="Pro Combat Advice">
Don't rely solely on one Soul Switch. Different bosses in AGE 1000 have specific weaknesses that can be exploited more effectively by switching your soul mid-battle to counter their mechanics.
</Callout>
